Where we go

Coverage across 15 countries

From our Kraków base we deliver to 15 European countries: Poland, Germany, Czechia, Austria, Italy, France, Monaco, Spain, Andorra, Portugal, Belgium, the Netherlands, Luxembourg, Denmark, and Sweden. Whether it is a domestic delivery or a multi-country run, we plan the most efficient route and keep you updated the whole way.

  • Daily departures on major EU corridors
  • Full truckload (FTL) and groupage (LTL)
  • Last-mile delivery in key cities
